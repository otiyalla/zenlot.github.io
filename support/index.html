Help and support for Zenlot users

About Zenlot

Zenlot is a risk management tool designed to help traders plan trades, manage risk, and track trading performance more effectively.

Account Access and Sign-In Issues

If you cannot sign in to your account, ensure that you are using the email address associated with your Zenlot account.

Password Reset

To reset your password:

  1. Open the Zenlot app.
  2. Go to the Sign In page.
  3. Tap Forgot Password.
  4. Enter the email address you used when signing up.
  5. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

Bug Reports and App Performance Issues

If you encounter bugs, performance issues, or unexpected behavior in the app, you can report them directly within Zenlot.

To report a bug or provide feedback:

  1. Open the Zenlot app.
  2. Navigate to Profile.
  3. Scroll to the Account Actions section.
  4. Select Give Feedback.
  5. You can submit:
    • Bug reports
    • Performance issues
    • Feature requests
    • Questions
    • General feedback

Additional Support

If you are unable to resolve your issue through the app, you may contact Zenlot support directly.

Email: info@zenlot.net
