Zenlot Account Deletion

How to request account and data deletion

Delete Your Zenlot Account

Users can request deletion of their Zenlot account and associated data directly within the app. This page is provided for Google Play Store compliance requirements.

Steps to delete your account
  1. Open the Zenlot app.
  2. Tap the Profile button in the bottom menu.
  3. Open Account Actions.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the confirmation steps shown in the app.

Delete Trade Journals and Trade Logs

Users may delete individual trade journals and trade logs at any time by clicking the Delete button on the specific entry.

Data Deleted

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account profile data
  • Trade journals
  • Trade logs
  • User-generated account data

Data That May Be Retained

Certain records may be temporarily retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or backup recovery purposes.

  • Security logs
  • System backup copies
  • Compliance-related records

Retention Period

Deleted data is removed from active systems after processing. Backup and security-related records may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion unless otherwise required by law.
